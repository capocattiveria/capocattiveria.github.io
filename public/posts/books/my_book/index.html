This is a list of my books. I haven’t read all of them yet, and perhaps I won’t read some of them at all. The ones labeled as e-books were read on the Kindle. I might share my thoughts on some that I particularly enjoyed. It’s always nice to browse through the shelves of others, isn’t it? Perhaps through a library, one can catch a glimpse of a person’s essence because books are chosen out of curiosity, and I believe that curiosity somewhat defines us.
